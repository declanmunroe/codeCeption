Continuous Professional Development (CPD) for IT professionals

With CPD from the Irish Computer Society you can:

  • Take ownership of your career development.
  • Record your CPD activities for verification.
  • Track and review your CPD progress.

The Irish Computer Society has developed for its members a free-to-use, online system to support their tracking of their professional development.

CPD from the Irish Computer Society is inspired by the work of the Council of European Professional Informatics Societies (CEPIS), in particular its landmark study prepared for the European Commission, e-Skills and ICT professionalism: Fostering the ICT Profession in Europe.

CPD for IT professionals:
Now in Ireland. Now for you.
